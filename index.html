What is an Agentic OS?

Think of OpenSwarm as the Linux of autonomous agent systems. Just as Linux provided a universal kernel for software, OpenSwarm provides the runtime, coordination layer, and governance model for AI agents to operate at scale.

Agents are not tools — they are peers. OpenSwarm enables them to discover each other, negotiate task assignments, share context, and self-organize into swarms that solve complex problems collaboratively.
